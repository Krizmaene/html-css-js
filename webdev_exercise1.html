UI/UX Design Details

This is our final project in the course of Human Computer Interaction (HuCoInt). We made a high-fidelity wireframe of our application called HomeCurate. HomeCurate is an application that allows users to create and customize the interior and exterior of their house. They can try different room styles, wall designs, house decor, exterior designs, and more choices that they prefer having in their house area.

Ready, Selfie, Go! Details

This is an activity in our course Contemporary World (CoWorld). We were tasked to take pictures inside the campus that show and describe the difference between the Global North and the Global South. Global North countries have high-income economies and advanced technological infrastructure, while Global South countries face challenges like poverty.

Snack Shack Details

This is the logo of our final project in the subject Entrepreneurship when I was in Grade 12. Our business, called Snack Shack, sold scramble, churros, cinnamon rolls, and iced coffee. We won three awards: 1st Place for Most Promising Young Entrepreneur, 2nd Place for Service Awardee, and 3rd Place for Best Marketing Strategy.
